At Dubai Duty Free Exchange Bureau (DDF-EB), our Exchange Bureau operations are centred on delivering seamless, fast, and customer-focused currency exchange services across our strategically located airport counters. Operating within one of the world’s busiest travel hubs, we serve a diverse global clientele with professionalism, accuracy, and care.

We are a licensed exchange house, authorised by the Central Bank of the UAE (CBUAE), and operate in full compliance with all regulatory requirements.
